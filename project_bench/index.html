bench
Industrial design Prototyping CAD/CAM

A two-person bench constructed from 3mm plywood and assembled using zip ties. The design features a clean, minimalist aesthetic, with flowing curves that enhance both its visual dynamism and structural integrity. It includes an easy-to-follow assembly manual, emphasizing accessible construction and lightweight, tool-free assembly.

As a complementary element, a desk lamp was designed to accompany the bench, using three materials to project shifting light patterns through a rotating ring. Though secondary in function, the lamp echoes the bench’s curved geometry and material language, reinforcing a unified and expressive design approach.
