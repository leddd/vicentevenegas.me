Blitz is an edible chess set made with filled cookies, where two distinct flavors represent each side of the board. Designed to offer a playful and casual introduction to chess, it invites users to engage with the game without the pressure of full commitment. By combining food and play, Blitz creates a relaxed and social experience centered around the snack table.

Cabrón is a branding and packaging concept for a fictional charcoal brand. The project includes the design of an imagotype, product packaging, and potential line extensions. With a bold, irreverent tone, Cabrón reimagines a utilitarian product through a sharp visual identity and cohesive graphic system that stands out on the shelf.

At Almabrands, I contributed to the design of brand identities for Adelco and the Angelini Foundation, working on logo development and the application of visual systems across various touchpoints. These projects involved translating strategic concepts into cohesive, adaptable design solutions.
