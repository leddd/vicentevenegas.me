Product design Interaction design CAD/CAM

A DIY kit that lets students build a low-cost, hands-on device to explore sound recording and playback. It doesn’t rely on traditional instruments or advanced software—instead, it invites users to experiment with audio using basic electronics, open-source tools, and creative thinking.

The kit is designed as an educational tool to support STEAM learning and introduce students to the fundamentals of sound production. It encourages exploration in music, electronics, and programming, all while making the technical process visible and understandable. Whether used in classrooms or extracurricular workshops, it helps democratize access to music-making, empowering students to create, experiment, and understand the tools behind the sound.
